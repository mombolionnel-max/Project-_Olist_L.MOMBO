## Description

Brazil represents the largest e-commerce market in Latin America with a population of 213 million inhabitants. 
an ultra-connected population where the smartphone is largely ingrained in consumption habits. In this immense 
country with a unique geography and an economic disparity between the different segments of the population Brazil 
presents unique logistical and financial challenges. Olist (is a Brazilian e-commerce company founded on July 15 2015 in Curitiba) 
attempts to meet this challenge.  his business model is a marketplace focused on providing technology solutions for e-commerce, 
primarily for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in Brazil. In this project, i try to identify some key indicators of the company.
